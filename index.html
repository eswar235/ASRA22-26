Our Story

Four Years.
One Family.

It started with nervous smiles and unfamiliar faces in the corridors of Avanthi's. We were strangers from different towns, different backgrounds — united only by a branch code and a shared dream.

Through sleepless nights before exams, chaotic lab sessions, spontaneous trips, and 3AM chai conversations — we didn't just earn a degree. We built something far more precious: each other.

ASRA CSE 2022–2026 isn't just a batch. It's a chapter of life we'll carry forever — in every city we move to, every job we take, every milestone we celebrate.

Our Journey

The Timeline

🌱
2022

The Beginning

New city. New faces. Hostel rooms that smelled like instant noodles and anxiety. We had no idea what we were doing — and that was the best part. 64 strangers walked into Avanthi's and a family was born.

🤝
2023

Friendship Grows

Lab sessions turned into late-night study groups. Canteen breaks turned into life conversations. We stopped being classmates and started being friends — the kind that feel like home.

🔥
2024

Strong Bonds

Hackathons, college fests, trips, and shared dreams. We built projects together, failed together, celebrated together. The bond became unbreakable. This was our golden year.

🚀
2025

Placement Season

The anxiety, the prep, the midnight calls — "I got it!" Placement season was chaos and triumph all at once. We cheered for each other louder than we cheered for ourselves.

🎓
2026

Farewell

We came as strangers. We leave as family. Four years of memories, laughter, tears, and growth. This isn't goodbye — it's see you on the other side. Always ASRA. Always family.
